#### What is EPFO?

EPFO is responsible for the administration of the Employees' Provident Fund and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952. It aims to promote retirement savings for employees and provide social security benefits.

#### Key Schemes under EPFO

*   Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) Scheme, 1952: A savings scheme where both the employer and employee contribute a certain percentage of the employee's salary.
*   Employees' Pension Scheme (EPS), 1995: Provides pension benefits to employees after retirement.
*   Employees' Deposit Linked Insurance (EDLI) Scheme, 1976: Provides insurance coverage to employees.

#### What is PF Consulting?

PF consulting involves professional services aimed at assisting employers and employees in handling all aspects of the Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF). Consultants provide advisory and operational support to ensure compliance with EPFO regulations, timely filing of returns, and error-free claim management.

#### Services Offered by PF Consultants

PF consultants offer a range of services, including:

*   **Compliance Management:** Ensuring that organizations adhere to EPFO rules and regulations.
*   **PF Registration and Account Setup:** Assisting employers in registering with EPFO and setting up employee accounts.
*   **Monthly Contribution Support:** Helping employers calculate and file monthly contributions accurately.
*   **PF Claims Assistance:** Guiding employees through the process of partial or full withdrawal of their PF.
*   **Audit and Inspection Support:** Helping organizations prepare for audits and inspections by the EPFO.
*   **Advisory Services:** Offering advice on reducing compliance risks and improving PF management.
