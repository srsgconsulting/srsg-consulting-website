## About SRSG Consulting

SRSG Consulting supports organizations across India with practical compliance and business solutions. Our focus is simple: make compliance easier, reduce risk, and keep your operations running smoothly.

### What we do

- **ESI** and **EPFO** registration and ongoing compliance support
- **Factories Act** compliance guidance and documentation support
- **Compliance consulting** for day-to-day statutory requirements
- **Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)** support for filings
- **Health insurance** advisory for organizations and employees
- **Digital marketing** support for business growth

### Our approach

- Clear guidance with actionable steps
- Transparent process and timelines
- Reliable support for recurring compliance needs
